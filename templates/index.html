Welcome to our official website.
We are a Community Development Association (CDA) Registered in Ogun State, Nigeria. The principal aim of our CDA is to ensure rapid development of the community by providing a forum which would make it possible for the people to show high sense of responsibility in the social and economic programmes of the community as a whole. As a result of this, it would be easy to promote wise initiative, encourage responsible leadership and ensure fullest participation of the people
